About AG Telecom LLC

Established in 2006 in Dubai, AG Telecom LLC invests in the telecom sector in emerging markets where infrastructure gaps create rapid growth opportunities.

Our operating subsidiary, Wasel Telecom, holds a national Mobile Network Operator (MNO) license in Afghanistan and valuable 4G spectrum in the B5 band (850 MHz).

Shareholders are actively seeking strategic partners for a majority stake transaction and new capital to support network expansion.
